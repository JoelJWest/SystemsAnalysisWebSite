Our Company

Founded in 1996, Panther Professional Consulting has helped customers all over the world use information technology to increase business productivity. With industry leading experience, and a world-class team, our company is dedicated to bringing your business the tools it needs to succeed in the age of information. Our expert staff can provide many services including, business process analysis, systems design as well as complete information system implementation. Our core mission has been to bring the latest technology to our customers while giving them the skills they need to maximize their effectiveness.
